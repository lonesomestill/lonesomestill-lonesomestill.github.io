Garrett Massman
Garrett Massman got his start on electric guitar but was eventually corrupted by his bluegrass roots in Signal Mountain, TN. He brings a twang to his songwriting, guitar, dobro, mandolin, programming code, and software documentation.
Ian Kelmartin
Ian comes to the Bay by way of Humboldt County, CA, hailing originally from PA. Versed on Bass and Guitar, his songs are inspired by good beer, nature, and fish - after all, if he stuck to his day job he’d clam up! While the band shares musical leadership, Ian is known as the heart of the band, bringing everyone together.
Michelle Yu
Michelle Yu is a California native who has been playing violin before even the band can remember! A converted classical musician, the fiddle provides a natural outlet for her day job saving lives. She can be found strumming some old time tunes, clogging away at an Old Time Fest', or corralling friends into a country line dance.
Nat Hendel
Nat Hendel is a true local, coming out of Walnut Creek, CA. Picking up mandolin before it was cool to have a pandemic hobby, Nat is know for his “mandolines”, that is a mix of badass playing with clever songwriting lyrics.
